Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information provided by users of the Telangana State Licensed Surveyors Community Portal.

Information We Collect

  • Personal information voluntarily provided by registered surveyors (name, contact details, license info).
  • Usage data to improve site performance and user experience.

How We Use Information

  • To verify and display licensed surveyors in Telangana.
  • To maintain the integrity and accuracy of the community directory.
  • To communicate updates or announcements to registered users.

Data Protection

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the data you provide. However, as this is a community-driven portal, users should verify information independently.

Third-Party Services

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties. Any external links are for informational purposes only.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the Telangana State Licensed Surveyors Community Portal ("TSLS Portal") collects, uses, and protects the information of its users.

1. Community-Driven Data

All surveyor information, including name, phone number, district, and mandal, is submitted by licensed surveyors themselves or verified by the community. We do not claim official government authority.

2. Data Usage

The information is used only for:

  • Displaying surveyor directories
  • Generating QR codes for verification
  • Community contact and networking purposes

3. Personal Information Protection

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties. Phone numbers are displayed publicly only for surveyor verification and community contact purposes.

4. Cookies & Analytics

This website may use minimal analytics to understand traffic and improve the user experience. No personal data is tracked without consent.

5. External Links

Our portal may link to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

6. Disclaimer

This is a community-driven portal and is not officially affiliated with the Telangana State Licensed Surveyors Association (TSLSA) or any government authority.

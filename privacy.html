Privacy Policy

Understanding how your data is collected and used on our community portal.

Welcome to the Telangana State Licensed Surveyors Community Portal. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. By using this website, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as when registering a professional record or contacting us. This may include name, email, phone number, district, and mandal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Maintain the community directory of licensed surveyors
  • Enable users to verify surveyor information via QR codes
  • Communicate important updates or announcements

3. Sharing Information

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties except where required by law or to provide services through the portal.

4. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies and analytics tools to improve user experience and understand site usage. No personal identification information is stored through analytics.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. However, no internet system is completely secure.

6. Updates to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Continued use of the portal indicates acceptance of any changes.
